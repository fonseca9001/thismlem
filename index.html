Ever Dream This Mlem?

Every night throughout the world, hundreds of people dream about this dumb face.

HISTORY



In March 2020, in Lisbon, a patient of a well-known psychiatrist drew the face of a man who had been repeatedly appearing in her dreams. On multiple occasions, This Mlem had led her through OSR adventures, referring to himself as Mlem. The woman insisted she had never encountered this "Mlem" in her waking life.
The portrait remained forgotten on the psychiatrist's desk for a few days until another patient recognized the face. He revealed that the man often expressed his deep fascination and love for NTR (Not Safe For Work content involving romantic partners being unfaithful) within his dreams. Strangely, the patient had never encountered this man in reality.
Intrigued, the psychiatrist decided to share the portrait with colleagues who had patients experiencing recurring dreams. Within a few months, four other patients identified the man as a frequent presence in their own dreams. In some instances, he was running Dungeon World games, while in others, he was drawing obscure and unconventional artwork. All these patients referred to him as THIS MLEM.
From August 19th 2019 to the present day, approximately 2000 individuals from the rpgportugal discord server have claimed to have encountered this enigmatic figure in their dreams, spanning various cities worldwide, including Los Angeles, Berlin, São Paulo, Tehran, Beijing, Rome, Barcelona, Stockholm, Paris, New Delhi, Moscow, and more.
Curiously, there is no discernible connection or common thread among those who have dreamed of encountering This Mlem. Furthermore, no living person has ever been identified as resembling the man depicted in the portrait by those who have seen him in their dreams.
The primary objectives of this website are:

  • To assist individuals who have dreamed of This Mlem in fostering communication among themselves.

  • To delve into the mystery of who This Mlem is and why he appears in such a seemingly random assortment of situations within the dreams of such diverse human subjects.

THEORIES



THE MYSTICAL MLEM THEORY
According to this theory, This Mlem is a mystical entity that transcends the earthly realm. It is believed that he is a messenger who communicates through dreams to guide the dreamers on a quest for existential mlemness.

THE META-MLEM THEORY
This theory proposes that This Mlem exists in a meta-dream realm, where he orchestrates bizarre dream scenarios to teach individuals about the whimsical nature of reality, making the dreamers question the fabric of their own existence.

THE MLEM COLLECTIVE THEORY
It is speculated that This Mlem is a collective dream character created from the collective unconscious of humanity. Whenever someone dreams of This Mlem, they contribute to the collective narrative, evolving the story of This Mlem in the dream realm.

THE MLEM RECURSION THEORY
This theory delves into the concept of dreams within dreams, proposing that This Mlem is a dream character who has gained self-awareness and is trying to navigate through the labyrinth of collective human dreaming to find his own reality.

THE COSMIC MLEM THEORY
Some believe that This Mlem is a cosmic jokester who plays with the minds of humans for amusement, while also providing cryptic insight into the cosmic mlemody of existence.
