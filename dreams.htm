DREAMS

In my dream, I was in a library with this mlem. He whispered in my ear the location of a secret ancient tome, which when read, would grant me the ability to fly. But before I could reach the book, I woke up... "In the haunting gloom of my dreams, a quirky figure named This Mlem emerged from the veil of shadows, holding an elaborate illustration in his hands. He asked softly if I could recognize my father in it. Even though the image bore no resemblance to anything familiar, I inexplicably agreed. Suddenly, the scene shifted, and I found myself standing before a grave marked 'Unknown'. Mlem, now donned in a flight attendant uniform, guided me through an ancient, overgrown pathway to a peaceful clearing. As I looked back, the grave seemed to sink into the earth, replaced by a serene garden where Mlem gracefully sketched the dawn breaking over the horizon."

"From the very first dream, I found myself enchanted by This Mlem. Despite his peculiar furry attire, the allure of his artistic talent was undeniable. We'd wander through whimsical meadows, where he’d sketch beautiful sceneries as we dined over a vegan feast. The twilight accentuated his every stroke, weaving a romantic tale that seemed to defy the grim reality I woke to each morning. His tender gestures left a warmth that lingered long after the dreams faded."

"The recurrent dreams of soaring through the skies took a surreal turn with This Mlem's presence. Adorned in a crisp flight attendant uniform, he'd join my flight, orchestrating our journey through surreal landscapes only seen in the intricate illustrations he’d often sketch. Although he never spoke, his calm demeanor was a comforting companion in the lonely expanse of the dream skies, making every flight a serene, unspoken adventure."

"During a particularly stressful period at work, I was thrust into a labyrinth of dreary cubicles in my dreams. Trapped in a seemingly endless maze, This Mlem appeared as a beacon of whimsy amidst the monochrome reality. His jests about unemployment lightened the eerie atmosphere, transforming the restrictive cubicles into open fields with each witty remark. As we navigated out of the grim maze, the anxiety of my waking life seemed to dissipate with the morning mist."

"The eerie dreams where I found myself entangled with This Mlem in vivid NTR scenarios were bewildering yet intriguing. Despite the bizarre setting, his witty humor brought a light-heartedness to the otherwise eerie dreamscape. His jests punctuated the surreal narrative, blurring the lines between fear and fascination, leaving me pondering the complex tapestry of emotions woven through the dream realm."

Despite my frantic attempts to escape, the bizarre narrative spiraled deeper into unsettling surrealism, each jest from Mlem a haunting echo in the chilling expanse."

"Caught in a nightmarish loop, This Mlem's visage on the screen was both a comfort and a curse.
