THEORIES

Several theories have been developed to explain the mysteriously recurring presence of This Mlem in the dreams of different people. The following theories are the ones that elicit the greatest interest among the dreamers themselves.

THE MYSTICAL MLEM THEORY
According to this theory, This Mlem is a mystical entity that transcends the earthly realm. It is believed that he is a messenger who communicates through dreams to guide the dreamers on a quest for existential mlemness.

THE META-MLEM THEORY
This theory proposes that This Mlem exists in a meta-dream realm, where he orchestrates bizarre dream scenarios to teach individuals about the whimsical nature of reality, making the dreamers question the fabric of their own existence.

THE MLEM COLLECTIVE THEORY
It is speculated that This Mlem is a collective dream character created from the collective unconscious of humanity. Whenever someone dreams of This Mlem, they contribute to the collective narrative, evolving the story of This Mlem in the dream realm.

THE MLEM RECURSION THEORY
This theory delves into the concept of dreams within dreams, proposing that This Mlem is a dream character who has gained self-awareness and is trying to navigate through the labyrinth of collective human dreaming to find his own reality.

THE COSMIC MLEM THEORY
Some believe that This Mlem is a cosmic jokester who plays with the minds of humans for amusement, while also providing cryptic insight into the cosmic mlemody of existence.
